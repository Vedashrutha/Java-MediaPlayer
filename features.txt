Features:
- Supports both audio and video files.
- Control playback rate of the media.
- Volume boost support for a better experience.
- Support for background audio/video playback.
- Support for fullscreen media playback.
- Use available controls to control the playback of the media.
- Lightweight app.
- Requires less storage and memory.
